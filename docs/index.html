HAMBoard Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026

HAMBoard is a tvOS application developed by an independent developer for amateur radio enthusiasts. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through the HAMBoard app.

Information We Collect

HAMBoard does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. The app fetches publicly available data from third-party sources to provide features such as DX Cluster spots, propagation conditions, and contest calendars. This data includes:

  • Solar and propagation information from services like hamqsl.com and prop.kc2g.com.
  • Contest details from contestcalendar.com.
  • Country data from country-files.com.
  • Live DX Cluster feeds via Telnet from configurable public endpoints.

No user-specific data, such as device identifiers, location, or personal details, is collected or processed by the app. All data retrieved is anonymous and used solely for display within the app.

How We Use Information

Since no personal information is collected, there is no usage of such data. The app processes fetched public data in real-time for on-device rendering and does not retain it after the session ends.

Sharing of Information

We do not share any information with third parties because no personal or user data is collected.

Data Security

As no personal data is handled, there are no specific security measures required for user information. However, the app uses standard secure protocols (e.g., HTTPS for web requests and Telnet for DX clusters) to fetch public data.

Children's Privacy

HAMBoard does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age, as no data is collected at all.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on the app's GitHub repository (https://github.com/Krusty84/HAMBoard), and the effective date will be updated accordingly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App's data practices, please contact us at sedoykin@gmail.com.
